By age 50, about eighty five percent of men will experience some aspects of hair thinning. Here are five foods that help you prevent this. Number one, eggs. Eggs are full of biotin and protein that help your hair to grow. Number two is gelatin. The collagen in gelatin keeps your scalp skin healthy and it promotes hair growth. Number three is oysters. Oysters are full of zinc which is an important mineral needed for hair growth and follicles. Number four, beef. Beef is also an excellent source of zinc but it has protein as well. And number five is berries. The antioxidants in berries protect your hair from oxidative stress. Before you spend a lot of money on different kinds of hair products, make sure you add these foods into your diet.
