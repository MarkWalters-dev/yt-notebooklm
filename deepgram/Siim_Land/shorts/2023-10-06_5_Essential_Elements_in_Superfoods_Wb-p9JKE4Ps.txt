Don't know what makes a superfood really super? Here are five elements that are beneficial in commonly known superfoods. Number one, boron. Many people aren't getting enough boron which is important for hormones and bones. To get some boron, you would want to eat eat some dried prunes, raisins or different kinds of vegetables. Number two is chromium. Chromium is a mineral that helps to lower blood sugar levels and reduce cholesterol. Start eating foods like oysters and clams that are high in chromium. Number three, inositol. Inositol helps to lower blood sugar levels as well and grapefruit is very high in inositol. Number four, apigenin. Apigenin is a compound in herbs that has anti inflammatory benefits and it might help with raising your NAD levels. Parsley is very high in apigenin and is very easy to add to your meals. Number five, essential minerals. Mineral waters contain a lot of different kinds of minerals. They help to lower your blood sugar levels, lower inflammation, lower joint pain and reduce stress. By implementing these foods into your diet, you can see improvements in your health and wellness.
