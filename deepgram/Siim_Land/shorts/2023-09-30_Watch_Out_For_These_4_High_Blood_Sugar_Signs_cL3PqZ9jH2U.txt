Don't know if your blood sugar is creeping up? There are four common signs to look out for. Number one, thirst and dehydration. Number two, a constant need to pee, especially during the night. Number three, blurred vision. Number four, neuropathy. This is where you feel the tingling in your toes and fingers. Diabetes is a common medical condition that about fifteen percent of people will have. But the concerning thing is that up to forty percent of people don't know that they have poor glucose levels. Start paying attention to your blood sugar levels and watch out for these signs.
