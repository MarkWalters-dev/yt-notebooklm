If you want to be healthy then you should eat these five foods every day. Number one, eggs. They're full of choline and essential amino acids that are beneficial for the brain and overall muscle growth. Eggs have also been found to be very satiating and beneficial for weight loss. Number two, olive oil is high in polyphenols and oleic acid that have anti inflammatory and antioxidant benefits. Olive oil has been found to be beneficial for the brain and heart. Number three, green tea is full of antioxidants and polyphenols which have been found to reduce the risk of diabetes and heart disease. Number four, coffee is also full of polyphenols and has been found to be associated with reduced mortality and reduced cardiovascular disease risk. You can't drink too much coffee because otherwise you're gonna get too much caffeine. The sweet spot is two to three cups per day. And lastly, number five is raw cacao or just dark chocolate. Cacao is full of polyphenols and has been also found to be associated with reduced heart disease.
