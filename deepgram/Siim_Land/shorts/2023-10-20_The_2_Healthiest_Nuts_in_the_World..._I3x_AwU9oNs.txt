Everyone says you should eat some nuts but which ones are the healthiest? Start eating these two nuts for a healthier diet. Number one. Brazil nuts. Brazil nuts are the world's highest source of selenium which is an important mineral for your immune system. System. You can cover your entire daily selenium requirement with only two to three Brazil nuts. But this is also the reason why you don't want to eat too many Brazil nuts because you might get selenium poisoning. Don't eat any more than four Brazil nuts a day. Number two is walnuts. Walnuts look like a brain and they're said to be beneficial for the brain, but walnuts are actually one of the highest sources of dietary polyphenols in the world. This makes them excellent for your heart health, your blood vessels, and your brain. So if you only add these two nuts into your diet, then you will see an improvement in your health.
