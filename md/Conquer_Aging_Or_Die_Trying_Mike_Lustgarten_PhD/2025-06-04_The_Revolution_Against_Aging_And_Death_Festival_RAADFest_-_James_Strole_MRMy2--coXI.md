# 2025-06-04 The Revolution Against Aging And Death Festival RAADFest - James Strole

## Executive Summary

*   **Radical Life Extension Community Growth:** RAADFest has successfully fostered a global longevity community, evidenced by over a thousand attendees from 30+ countries at its inaugural 2016 event, and has facilitated collaboration and funding for longevity initiatives.
*   **Scientific and Inspirational Synergy:** The event emphasizes the powerful combination of cutting-edge science and inspiration, believing that embracing the possibility of unlimited lifespans strengthens personal resolve and enhances the effectiveness of health innovations.
*   **Unprecedented Collaboration:** The 2025 RAADFest features an unprecedented collaboration with the LongeCity Foundation and Aubrey de Grey, integrating his renowned longevity summit research with advanced clinical practices.
*   **Longevity Lounge Clinic Innovations:** A new "Longevity Lounge Clinic" at RAADFest offers attendees discounted access to cutting-edge longevity innovations, including gene therapy, plasma exchange, exosome administrations, and advanced diagnostics, with opportunities for follow-up consultations.
*   **Deeper Scientific Exploration:** The "Longevity Labs" format allows for more in-depth presentations on specific topics like stem cells and exosomes, running concurrently with the main stage to offer attendees broader choices and interaction opportunities.

## Event Details

*   **Name:** Revolution Against Aging and Death Festival (RAADFest)
*   **Date:** July 10, 2025
*   **Location:** Red Rock Resort, Las Vegas
*   **Organizer:** Coalition for Radical Life Extension (a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization)
*   **Affiliation:** A global outreach initiative with People Unlimited (a longevity community in Scottsdale, Arizona).

## History and Mission of RAADFest

*   **Origin of Idea:** Conceptualized in 2014 at People Unlimited to reach out to the growing community of longevity researchers and physicians.
*   **Formation of Coalition:** The Coalition for Radical Life Extension was formed with the goal of creating a platform for collaboration and inspiration in the radical life extension field.
*   **Inaugural Event (2016):**
    *   Record-breaking attendance for an event of its kind, with over 1,000 participants from 30+ countries.
    *   Established a global longevity community.
    *   Attracted funding for longevity initiatives.
    *   Generated significant international press, helping to overcome prior skepticism about longevity research.
    *   Enabled and accelerated collaboration between researchers, entrepreneurs, advocates, and the public.
*   **Nonprofit Status and Ethics:**
    *   RAADFest is a nonprofit organization.
    *   Does not sell products directly or take percentages from exhibiting companies, avoiding conflicts of interest.
    *   Focuses on bringing the best available health and wellness innovations to attendees.
*   **Core Goals:**
    *   **Galvanize the Radical Life Extension Community:** Foster camaraderie and collaboration to accelerate progress.
    *   **Create a Sea Change in the Longevity Movement:** Promote openness, collaboration, and a lack of competition with other organizations or companies in the field.
    *   **Blend Science and Inspiration:** Emphasize the synergistic power of scientific advancements and a positive, embracing mindset towards longevity.

## Key Themes and Concepts

*   **Synergy of Science and Inspiration:** The belief that embracing the possibility of unlimited lifespans, rather than viewing it as science fiction, strengthens personal resolve, resilience, and emotional strength. Aligned mindset is crucial for the effectiveness of health innovations.
*   **Overcoming Isolation:** Many scientists and doctors in the longevity field feel isolated; RAADFest provides a crucial networking and community-building opportunity.
*   **Collaboration:** Emphasis on working together, with gratitude extended to other organizations like the ones led by Brian Johnson, as collective effort accelerates progress.
